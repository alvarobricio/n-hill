A bit about us

We’re all about bringing you the very best of London, whether you’re a born-and-bred local or just visiting for the weekend. Our mission? To skip the tourist traps and make sure you have a proper brilliant time exploring our city.

Our Story

Back in 2018, Notting Hill Hopps started out as just two local mates, Álvaro and David, sharing their favourite West London haunts. Since then, we’ve grown into a full team of enthusiasts, showing travellers from across the globe the side of London you won't find in the guidebooks.

  • 2018: It all began with Álvaro and David and a big idea.
  • 2019: We hit the pavements with our first guided walking tours.
  • 2020: We branched out into private bike rides and food tours.
  • 2022: Made things easier with our new booking system and online reviews.

Why wander with us?

  • Expert local guides who know London’s nooks and crannies like the back of their hand.
  • Flexible timings that work around your plans, not the other way around.
  • A friendly, safe atmosphere that’s perfect for families and solo explorers alike.
  • Properly interactive experiences—less lecturing, more fun!
